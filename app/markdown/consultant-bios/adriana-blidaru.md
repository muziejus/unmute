Adriana Blidaru (b. Romania) is a contemporary art curator and writer based in
New York. Her research meets at the intersection of environmental studies,
animal rights, decolonization, diaspora, and gender studies. She is the
founding editor of [_Living Content_](https://www.livingcontent.online/), a
curatorial platform that features interviews with artists, exhibition
recommendations,  limited editions, events and exhibitions. Adriana holds an
MA in Curatorial Studies from Bard College and a BFA from the University of
Oxford.
